Our Vision

We equip the future generation with the tools and skills they need to create innovative solutions using technology. We empower youth to collaborate and break down social and economic barriers through identifying and solving issues they face in their communities using the power of tech.

We believe in breaking down the social and economic barriers to peace. Programmers 4 Peace hosts workshops, hackathons and events that equip individuals with the skills they need to take on social, political, and economic problems and improve lives worldwide. We encourage youth to challenge both simple and complex issues of their choice.
